List of ingredients to bake cake (UNOURDERED LIST)

  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Sugar
  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Baking powder

Steps to bake cake(ORDERED LIST)

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Mix flour, sugar, eggs, and butter in a bowl.
  3. Pour the batter into a greased baking pan.
  4. Bake for 30–35 minutes.
  5. Let it cool, then decorate and serve.
